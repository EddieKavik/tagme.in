Tag Me In

Robots welcome

As a public space on the internet, Tag Me In expressly welcome robots to contribute, vote on, or moderate objectionable content.

Tag Me In doesn't have designated human moderators. For that reason, robots are welcome to watch for new messages and programmatically remove any objectionable content.

Tag Me In is intended to be a repository of uplifting, positive messages. If a message is offensive to you or your organization, please remove it immediately using the following instructions.
